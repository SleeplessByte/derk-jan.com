---
title: Teriyaki tempeh
difficulty: easy
time: 30 minutes
vegan: true
---

import Ingredient from '../../components/Recipes/Ingredient';
import IngredientReference from '../../components/Recipes/IngredientReference';

1. Cut <Ingredient weight={200} unit="g">tempeh</Ingredient> in thin slices and then cut them in half.
2. Heat <Ingredient volume={4 * 15} unit="ml" simple:amount={4} simple:unit="tablespoon">olive oil</Ingredient> in a frying pan.
3. Fry the <IngredientReference>tempeh</IngredientReference> for 2 minutes per side until they turn brown and feel crispy.
4. Take the <IngredientReference>tempeh</IngredientReference> out of the pan and store in a bowl padded with kitchen paper to remove excess oil.
5. Remove as much oil as possible from the frying pan.
6. Re-heat the <IngredientReference>tempeh</IngredientReference> together with <Ingredient volume={4 * 15} unit="ml" simple:amount={4} simple:unit="tablespoon">teriyaki sauce</Ingredient> for approximately 2 minutes.
